Welcome!

I'm Hana , and welcome to my personal website! I'm a CS PhD student broadly interested in different aspects of systems & networking, machine learning, and algorithms. My recent focus is on AI-driven wireless communications and networking. More specifically, my research aims to improve IEEE 802.11 WLANs design in MAC and PHY layers. Prior to my PhD program, I worked as research assistant in LIG lab in Grenoble, France and Columbia University in New York City, NY. For more information on my previous and ongoing projects please visit Projects page.

Fun fact: I have an identical twin! Her name is Haniyeh and she does research in (human) languages.
